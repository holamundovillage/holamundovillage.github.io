Mission and Vision

We're on a mission to create a kickass space where the Spanish-speaking crew steals the spotlight, hooking everyone up with top-notch content, no strings attached.

To make it happen, we're keeping it real with content strictly in Spanish, and we've got English translations on standby. It's not just about breaking language barriers; it's about throwing a party for cultural diversity. We're throwing both languages in the mix, not just tipping our hats but giving a big shout-out to all the different perspectives that spice up our cybersecurity community. We all know that Spanish is the second most spoken language in the United States and 41.7 million people have Spanish as their native language.

We exist and we want to make our voices heard.

Why Choose Us

We're a squad of folks rocking Spanish as our mother tongue, each bringing our own flavor to the cybersecurity game. Our vibe? Sharing knowledge and blowing up the community, especially for those chilling on the fringes of the usual cybersecurity scene. When you roll with us, you're diving into a scene that's all about embracing diversity, keeping it real, and making cybersecurity accessible to everyone.
